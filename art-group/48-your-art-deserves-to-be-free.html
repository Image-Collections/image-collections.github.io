YOUR ART DESERVES TO BE FREE....

 

JUST ASK YOURSELF:

Hmmmm,.......I wonder if others might enjoy that story i've been slowly piecing together in my head?....

Odds are, if YOU'VE found it interesting enough to revisit time & time again in your head [and maybe even a scribble here or there on paper :o], then so will many others, who will be grateful you actually decided to share your talent...

 

.....It doesn't take much, but it could mean a LOT.... 

 

Free yourself, you DESERVE to tell your stories....and sequential art is one of the easiest ways to do it.....
