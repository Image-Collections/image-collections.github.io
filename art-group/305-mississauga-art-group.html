Mississauga Art Group

Mississauga Art Group @ Image Collections in Streetsville 

       Image Collections ART GROUP is made up of writers & artists who regularily meet to work on projects, host one of Canada's largest and longest-running PARKING LOT Free Comic Book Day Events, create comic-themed days, host multi-annual on-the-street COMIC-FESTs, take part in 24HR COMIC BOOK DAYs, have locally-based comic-flavoured Halloween Events every year, and are constantly doing outreach programs into the community to promote the industry....including the Mississauga Comic Book Expo MCX .....
