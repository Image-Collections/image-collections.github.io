                              <ABOVE>  ADONIS HOWARD & 'PAPA' LEE TRAHAN    [ among MANY Artists ] meet & greet while drawing up a storm @ ENCORE THEATERS [ FILM.CA CINEMAS ] in Oakville, Ontario at one of Comic Store Image Collections AMAZING SPIDER-MAN 2 Movie Events.....Where YOU there?

   A few quick pictures from one of our ART GROUP's MOVIE EVENTS for 'AMAZING SPIDER-MAN 2'...

   ....THIS ONE was @ ENCORE THEATERS in OAKVILLE [Kerr St/QEW Highway area], now called FILM.CA CINEMAS who provide the giant movie screens especially well-suited for comic-based movies....Encore offers both regular & 3-D versions [when available] of most film hits!

        A larger than expected turnout came out to see amazingly the 5th BIG-BUDGET SPIDER-MAN MOVIE in the last 13 years [ 3 'Tobys' + 2 'Garfields' ], who would have predicted THAT in 1998 when MARVEL was in bankruptcy, keeping all the artists hard at work answering comic-questions, sketching, & taking requests....

HUGE THANK YOUs go out to MIKE, ADONIS, LEE, WENDY, and many others for the great time had by all....

Big Thanks to TORI at 'ENCORE' for these first photos from the Event!...

The ENCORE THEATERS are located @ 171 Speers Road, Unit 25 in Oakville!

"If Shakespeare and Michelangelo were alive today, and if they decided to collaborate on a comic, Shakespeare would write the script and Michelangelo would draw it. How could anybody say that this wouldn't be as worthwhile an artform as anything on earth?" ----Stan Lee

       Image Collections ARTS GROUP is made up of writers & artists who regularily meet to work on projects, host one of Canada's largest and longest-running PARKING LOT Free Comic Book Day Events, create comic-themed days, host multi-annual on-the-street COMIC-FESTs, take part in 24HR COMIC BOOK DAYs, have locally-based comic-flavoured Halloween Events every year, and are constantly doing outreach programs into the community to promote the industry....
