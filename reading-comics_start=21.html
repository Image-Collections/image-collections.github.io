All New All Different Marvel Now #1

All New All Different Marvel Now #1 ----- All New All Different Marvel Now #1 ----- All New All Different Marvel Now #1

      MARVEL COMICS has, it seems, been leading it's readers on a journey of epic proportions over the past few years, with mighty characters taking 'the-big-dirt-nap', mysterious alliances poping up, & saga-inducing storylines taking its readers on quests never before imagined within the scope of what the long-loved 'MARVEL UNIVERSE' had been known as throughout its 55+ YEAR HISTORY with many of its flagships having existed enchanting [...through previous companies...] for over 75 YEARS!!!...

     ....Now, it seems likely those sometimes innocent times of yore, or Marvel-Lore, will no longer be shining down upon 'The House That STAN LEE & JACK KIRBY Built' [...among others...] as a storm of electicity will be shocking the company from storm clouds blacker than anything seen in its pages can relate to....

     ....30+ YEARS AGO, in out of a direction by then Editor-In-Chief Jim Shooter, an idea was hatched to shake up the heroes, adding new dramas, new costumes, and closer continuity for all in a project titled 'MARVEL SUPERHEROES SECRET WARS', its largest undertaking for the publisher at the time...

     ....It was HUGE, making many changes which still are affecting today's 'M.U.'!  So, flash-forward to 2015, where a NEW THREAT EMERGED from a simpler-titled sequel, 'SECRET WARS', which again has shifted the tectonic plates of the place so many have called home, while those ENGAGED-LOYAL-READERS looking in have been put to work trying to solve its thrilling puzzles, & decipher the rich, complex stories interlinked within....

    ....Now, FOR THE FIRST TIME IN ITS HISTORY, MARVEL COMICS IS 'STARTING OVER' [LINK #2] with drastically obvious examples of its 'ALL-NEW, ALL-DIFFERENT, ALL #1' tagline, where what you see, is not what you expect, nor what you know....

    .....It IS a FANTASTIC-JUMPING-ON-POINT for those newly converted, through the 'SECRET WARS' hype, the close-to-the-chest examples of the comics best storylines used for its blockbuster movies introducing millions to their heroes, the terrific TV offered-up pathway in, its videogame logged extensive biographical info peeking your curiousity, or just finally achieved the 'now-I'm-ready-to-be-taken-on-a-breathtaking-ride' state of mind needed to dip your toe in....

     

    .....ANY REASON IS ENOUGH, & worthy of picking up what MARVEL COMICS hasn't offered anyone in half a century, that is A PLACE TO BEGIN [Link #1], where indeed IT IS A START, for everything & everyone, both fiction or not, heroic or not, gender-friendly or not, & TRADITION OR NOT.....

    .....This is not about what it isn't, the same, it is instead about what IT IS, a MASSIVE OPPORTUNITY PROVIDED to be able to look back upon this FRESH BEGINNING as a WELCOMED PLACE TO STAND among THE HISTORY of the medium of sequential arts, from its GOLDEN AGE of Flash Gordon, Prince Valiant, & Superman, to the SILVER AGE which introduced more realism into the mix with BARRY ALLEN, aka THE FLASH, Peter Parker as SPIDER-MAN, & Tony Stark's IRON MAN, to name a few...

    .....From whatever STARTING POINTS [Link #1] lead you to PEEK INSIDE @ THE ARTFORM, to the JOYS OF SHARING WITH FRIENDS the dramas of each episode, & the CHANGING DISCUSSIONS around the watercooler, it takes little sluething to uncover THE TRUTH, which is times have changed, and both fustrating & JOYFULLY we're glad they have!...

   

      ....No longer can you 'count' on 'comic book storytelling' to be what it once was, as echoed throughout years of LOVING THESE CHARACTERS ALMOST AS MUCH AS THE MEDIUM ITSELF, readers have advanced into creators, and creators into editors caretaking the best & evolving the rest, making it A BETTER PRODUCT, a MORE WORTHY & DEEPER ENTERTAINMENT, & A WEALTHIER INDUSTRY for its indulgers....one grandly PROGRESSIVE & INVITING TO 'MARVEL AT'......pun intended!....

     ....No matter how once feels about a polarizing RE-LAUNCH of the ENTIRETY OF MARVEL COMICS, the industry will benefit, NEW FANS WILL BE BORN, & THE CREATIVITY WILL CONTINUE TO SHINE within this GROWING place where SO MANY ARE ALLOWED TO SHINE, allowed to SHARE, & allowed to FATHER [...& MOTHER...] this EXTRA-SIZED stable of nooks, crannies, & places where READERS & ARTISTS & WRITERS & UNIVERSES OF POSSIBILITY can ALL FEEL JUST AS AT HOME as most of us have felt CUDDLED UP WITH A GREAT BOOK.....

    ....YES, gone may be the days when staples in a story meant it was hindered by the trappings of a fearful watchdog [ see the 'joys' of the COMICS-CODE [Link #3] ] but indeed we now offer to the world everything & more of which any medium provides its users.....IDEAS....libraries of them, books of them, pages of them, & most importantly, panels of them....

     .....These soon-2-B new recruits will be joined by the newly nostalgic pangs of those whos movie-theater-watching JEDI were always ideally packaged & labelled as 'Episodes'....yet surely as HOTH has snow, they ARE just as passionate as any others!....

     .....As the story goes, "A LONG TIME AGO, IN A GALAXY devoid of Guardians looking like raccoons teaming up with speech-impeeded mobile trees, FAR FAR AWAY...", a certain GEORGE LUCAS, he later of HOWARD THE DUCK movie fame, had a tough time convincing the 'Powers That Be' @ MARVEL COMICS to put out a comics adaption of his 'space-fantasy' fairy-tale which went on to become 'the greatest of all time'....

      ....It is almost unfathomable today to envision a time when presenting a comic to the world about "blasters, droids, & spacey light-swords" was a risky proposition, yet in debt we all are to the decision-makers who's green light would fast-track [1970s style] the debut of the original 'STAR WARS #1' an adaption published astoundingly a few months BEFORE THE MOVIE EVEN HIT, leading a reading audience to promote the merits of its adventures to all who'd listen...

Marvel's STAR WARS comics did quite well, almost single-handedly lending a new generation multiple reasons to pick up a comic book filled with all-new extended stories every month, and, along with being excited by swash-buckling sci-fi 'swordplay' in a ground-breaking assortment of formats including magazine, treasury, & pocket book-sized, it allowed Marvel superheroes to piggy-back along as well through ads & specials...

     ..The truth is, from the enlightening aspects of the  'STAR WARS SICKNESSES', through to the heartbreaks of its GRIEF [...many STILL TELL tall tales, to anyone who'll listen, of how a long-since-released movie's unfilled potentials lead to their loss of inocence... :o], THE FORCE IS STRONG in us, and we are all one....gleefully welcoming any & all side effects which may lead to....THE DARK SIDE...both of ourselves & the movies AND COMICS we so dearly LOVE!.....

      .......Hence, MARVEL COMICS NEW STAR WARS #1, the reclaiming of the franchise by it's original publisher, is the PERFECT Disney-lead lead-in to DECEMBER 2015's third generation STAR WARS trilogy....

 As Stated In The Title To This, THE WALKING DEAD is Indeed Breaking Bad Habits in Comic Zombieland Which Have Festered For Some For What Seems Like Forever....

[...and IS forever in the case of most people's lives reading this...] 

           During most of the life-span of those over a certain age there existed, until recently, a long-standing pre-conceived notion the medium of comics contained mostly content befitting general-rated enteratinment, no more than 'laymen' reading, simplistic & uncomplicated....

     We can hear your cries of "How Could They Think...?", "Didn't They Know About...?", & "They Must Not Have Read...!" already, but the truth is....it's not their fault...mostly...

.

Who Would Have Believed One of the Most Unbelievable [read 'unrealistic'] Notions of Graphic Fiction, OK...lets say fiction, That Of 'The Dishelvelled, Deteriorating, Back From The Dead, Now Haunting Humanity-Challenged Humans While Walking In A World Of Disarray & Disorder' Genre Mainly Kickstarted By The 1968 'Night Of The Living Dead' movie, and given hefty boosting from both the'28 Days Later...' film* and 2003's 'THE WALKING DEAD' on-going monthly title from publisher IMAGE, would lead to the decaying of some previously heavily cemented beliefs about North American SEQUENTIAL ARTWORK and the enjoyment of it...

Not only did a TV SHOW do justice to it's original comic-based material, opening the door for other 'live-actioners' to come ['ARROW', Marvel's 'S.H.I.E.L.D', Image's 'THIEF OF THIEVES' & 'POWERS'**], it lead to behavioural changes the graphic arts medium has been trying to trend for at least half a century, especially so since the demise of it's own real-life horror [dog & pony] show, THE COMICS CODE , a staple ball & chain of stapled four colour entertainment since 1954.... 

Now almost eleven years [and 130+ issues-you'll-want-to-read-without-spoilers-if-possible] later, its influence is almost unheard of....

THE WALKING DEAD has all but eliminated the stigma of 'child-like' which had stuck to the medium throughout most of the 20th century, allowing it to flourish to new standards, opening new storytelling windows to look through for previously boxed-in-readers....

Sure, comics had 'grown up' in the 1980s, with true applause trickling in from the mainstream arts world, yet it would be painstaking years later before a certain TV-SHOW made the world actually stand up & take notice of all its potentials....

   Obviously many regular titles and publishers have contributed to a matured definition within the pages of the mixture of layered art with words, notable being VERTIGO COMICS, but none on such a wide-scale-assault as IMAGE COMICS 'THE WALKING DEAD'...

Almost singlehandedly it's responsible for, in a short time, multiplying the readership for, accessablilty of, & acclaim towards the truely great masterpieces available as well as those yet-to-come in both the graphic novels & the comics worlds....

An unlikely occurence as any, 'ZOMBIES' have made it easy to jump into the artistic merit & diversity of a form of expression now unburdened by its previous donkey-like saddle....
