     The 5 DRIVE IN has long been a staple of the MISSISSAUGA / OAKVILLE border, an out-of-the-ordinary movie watching experience in a 'so-old-its-fresh' outside-the-box methodology mixing a long lost increased social openness encouraging interaction & creativity [seen in many a variation of the S.U.V. spawned mini 'tent-city'] with the laid-back times of yore!

     The experience comes across as welcoming as the warm friendly staff manning the diner-styled Snackbars, doling out traditional fare alongside the unconventional [..Pickle-on-a-stick, anyone?..:o], while providing the ambiance of THE place where hot popcorn was meant to be eaten....

      Everything about the theater screams nostalgia, seemingly able to invoke memories of past dates both of family & fun, of frolicks & fortune in a revival of a time few attendees, based on their ages, should even be able to fathom, yet embrace....

      As for film viewing, putting aside screens larger than most houses, not bringing up the   overwhelming desire to confirm the vast number of 'perfect spots to watch' [..including the ability to portably pick one..] how many theaters provide adjustable seating, flexable volume, natural air conditioning, a children's playground, a vollyball court, don't mind if you chat or use your cellphone, offer at least 2 MOVIES FOR THE PRICE OF ONE, and let you walk around, while giving you a pleasent versatile atmosphere....where, if desired, one can look straight up...at nature's own atmosphere....
