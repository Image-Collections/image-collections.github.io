Comics Distribution Suspended Due To Coronavirus

 

 

 MORE DETAILS TO COME....

 

 #staySAFE

 #

 

 

 

 

The main distributor of comics, Diamond halts shipments of all new comics due to coronavirus pandemic..... New comics, usually shipping every wednesday, known as ' new comic book day', will be put on hold while the world firgures out what to hold onto as it is gripped by a growth of those infected by a supervirus similar to the one in the just released comic, 'The Resistance', not related....we hope!  

No details as to when new comic book shipping will resume are known....The comic book industry has been put in a critical position because of covid-19, not unlike most of the world-wide economy....MORE DETAILS TO COME

   WE ENCOURAGE YOU to SHARE YOUR VOICE, through the idea of STAY SAFE COMICS because you matter....

If you don't agree, that's ok, because that's what communication, conversations, & COMICS ARE FOR, THE SHARING OF IDEAS, THOUGHTS, & OPINIONS, and most of us can relate to having those....but maybe not sharing them... in comics.....yet....  :)

Stay SAFE everyone, best wishes to you & yours, please stay informed about your health, be kind......

When we all do what we can, what we CAN do becomes so much more.... #StaySAFEcomics

Until then, we should all be #WatchingOutForHEROES... These days, they are not hard to find!

We encourage you to engage in #MakingCOMICS about your experiences....the future will thank you! [CLICK below}
