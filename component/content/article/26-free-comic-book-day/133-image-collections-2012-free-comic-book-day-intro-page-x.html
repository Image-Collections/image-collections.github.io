----------2012 FCBDay UPDATE #01.....WHAT is Free Comic Book Day?

Free Comic Book Day (FCBD) is an annual 'thank-you' to current readers and 'helper' for those who've only heard about the potential of SEQUENTIAL ART....

Enticing new creators as well as readers into the world of creativity, welcoming back those who've previously enjoyed, and offering a special holiday for fans, it occurs the FIRST SAT OF MAY every year...

......in 2012 terms, that's SAT MAY 5th :o]....

THE 'NOT-AS-SHORT'....... ;o)

We thought as long as we are showcasing THE BEST OF THE INDUSTRY, why not put a spotlight on the creators who will make up the foundation of the FUTURE of it....

As we read, we all know there are LEGENDARY WRITERS & ARTISTS [...as well as Inkers, Letterists, Colourists...] who built skyscraping bodies of work over decades which continue to inspire today's output, as well thousands of top-shelf collections and GRAPHIC NOVELS which continue to astound as best-sellers long after their release, but what is the cement that has made all of this possible?...

While billions worldwide [...that's right, the 'B' word!...] have experienced and LOVE the 'complete package' of art intermingled with words taking them into realms different than their own, and millions have laid down their hearts and sentiments on a panelled page to provide it, how is it REALLY so many have seen this medium as worthy as a place for their visions?...
