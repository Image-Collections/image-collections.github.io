THE SHORT.............
For over 10 years, We have made this day EXTRA-SPECIAL by offering even MORE out of a day already PACKED with reasons to get pumped about COMICS, a truely spectacular medium for telling the tales everyone wants to read....

....Image Collections FREE COMIC BOOK DAY Event is....

---One of the oldest & largest Free Comic Book Day Events in Canada...

---Only 20 minutes from Toronto & most of the GTA...

---Come on down every year for a Giant-Sized Celebration of the medium of sequential storytelling...

---------2012 FCBDay UPDATE #03..... 

We hope you are looking forward to our traditional....
 
 Image Collections FREE COMIC BOOK DAY Event on the...
 FRIST SATURDAY OF MAY [...in 2012 terms, that's MAY 5th :o]....
 
Again this year we will be inviting LOCAL ARTISTS & WRITERS to shine brightly in the spotlight, so if you know anyone who wishes to gain the attentions of a [likely] soon-2-B-adoring public, please pass this message along to them....
 
They can contact us directly via phone or email [...see LINKS above...], and we can provide them the information about attending as a guest.  REMEMBER: Everyone has creativity within, just some aren't as shy about showing it off....
 
We will have a number of activites running throughout the day, as always, and will have some schools involved as well...Again, if you know of a community organization which may be interested in taking part, have them contact us......
 
FREE COMIC BOOK DAY 2012 @ IMAGE COLLECTIONS no doubt will be memorable for many of the same reasons so many have kept coming back year after year supporting it.....
