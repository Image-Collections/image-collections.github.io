2012 FREE COMIC BOOK DAY EVENT @ Image Collections, Mississauga's COMIC & CARD Store!

We hope you are looking forward to our traditional FREE COMIC BOOK DAY Event on the FRIST SATURDAY OF MAY [...in 2012 terms, that's MAY 5th :o]....
 
Again this year we will be inviting LOCAL ARTISTS & WRITERS to shine brightly in the spotlight, so if you know anyone who wishes to gain the attentions of a [likely] soon-2-B-adoring public, please pass this email along to them....
 
They can contact us directly via phone or email [...see our website], and we can provide them the information about attending as a guest.  Everyone has creativity within, just some aren't as shy about showing it off....
 
We will have a number of activites running throughout the day, as always, and will have some schools involved as well...Again, if you know of a community organization which may be interested in taking part, have them contact us...
 
FREE COMIC BOOK DAY 2012 @ IMAGE COLLECTIONS no doubt will be memorable for many of the same reasons so many have kept coming back year after year supporting it.....
 
Check back here for MORE annoucements coming soon....
 
You make it what it is, so THANKS TO YOU, again, and in advance.... :o)
