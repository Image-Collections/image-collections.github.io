 *********** Stay SAFE COMICS #113 ********** 

Stay SAFE comics - Getting It Out Means Getting It Out!

#ReadTradeShareCOMICS

 Creating comic art & writing can be a singular-person activity, however no, it doesn't have to be that way...

Asking others to JOIN IN THE FUN can be helpful in 'a whole new world' way, for all of those participating, & they may be eager, however sometimes it is us that needs the convincing, as, for whatever reason, we can have the feeling others may not be interested...

Fair enough...just also consider the reality of 'feelings aren't facts', no matter who claims them. :o)

There are LOTS who enjoy seeing talent working in-the-process, as shown by the number of people who pour over scripts, sit-in on Twitch or Switch viewing, watch 'extra features' of the "making of...", or even get excited about quiz show, sports, & reality programing!

 Truth is EVERYONE LOVES watching a master at work & most even a #MASTERinTRAINING, & who is to tell you ( or them ) that you are not one of these?

Because drawing art or writing stories has traditionally ( & especially lately) been done void of others physically, it may be easy to forget, it was not that long ago most comic books were worked on in groups, in 'bullpen' style where everyone could see & interact with one another...

At the start of the medium's huge popularity, almost a full CENTURY ago, when the original idea of paying to reprint 'used' comic strips from newspapers [ <---paper printed news-blogs that sold millions EVERY DAY, kids!!], became too successful, others got in on it, creating almost overnight competitiion.

Stranger things have happened [& 'it' has its own ALL-NEW comic based on expanding the Netflix TV Show!...Just ASK US! ], though the reality of the origins of the modern day comic book go directly through this baffling fact:

The licensing fees to create content for comics from the recycled became so high, it became cheaper to pay artists to actually write & draw new stories for the now lucrative industry, suddenly opening up all sorts of new jobs in penciling, inking, colouring, editing, & writing duties...

Thus was born a place borne to ideas overflowing, steming from an influx of creative folk who, especially at the beginning, didn't always have the clout or power, yet held the passion for the tools of the trade, and knew how to trade those tools for the eventual greater health of the medium.

Stan Lee, the MARVELous one, & Jack 'King' Kirby are two examples [of thousands] who grew with comics, helped them change, & in this case had a huge impact from their origins near the birth of comics, through to essentially co-creating Marvel Comics (or most of it) in the early 1960s, bringing a zesty new feel to a product few still thought had legs sales-wise [...even 40 years in :o) ]

Stan played with the idea of creating a 'secret club' facade towards its readers, eventually convincing enough with his pizzazz, energy, & glossy treatment of Marvel's fanbase that not only was it plausible, it was a reality seldom seen in a product many then saw [due to the implications of THE COMICS CODE ], as talking down to its long-time friends, a problem which lurked even into the 21st century.

As artistic flares became commonplace, thanks much to the tremendous influence Will Eisner & co had, the giant room of artists was given more freedom to speak, share, & produce with the support of his fellow creators, many who would go on to teach & open schools on the craft, its history, & mystiques. 

 Asking close people if they have a bit of extra time [most do these days] to TALK, LISTEN, & / or HELP OUT can make a HUGE difference for all parties.... Sometimes we can convince ourselves others 'can't' when many times they 'will' & actually 'want to'...Many times it's easier done than thought, making 2-words into 1 - - 'COMFORT' + 'ABLE'... 

 

MAKING TOGETHER-COMICS Better & Stronger!

 *********** Stay SAFE COMICS #115 ********** 

Stay SAFE comics - Staying Safe & Healthy, In More Ways Than None!

#ReadTradeShareCOMICS

One of the most common experiences in 2020 was the shared conflicted emotions behind hearing increassingly, "Sorry, event cancelled due to Covid19!"

In itself, the fustrations of being confronted over so many strains placed over what used to be normal leads to questioning, "How would we even define Life anymore?"...

THIS is 'heady-stuff' to ponder...not for everyone, and certainly can encompass mass amounts of time & energy, two resources that, despite our uncanny ability to somehow manage binge-watching entire seasons of TV or podcasts, really IS in short supply...

With growth, education, & experience, we can see clearer the horizons of our lifetime, and the spatial awareness of how far we have travelled in it, &, with reflection, how far we wish yet to go...

Going past ourselves can be very problematic at times, as we never truly see all that there is or was about us, so it can be very hard to judge where our own 'boundaries' begin or end...

It is usually the past or the future where many of our greatest accomplishments are needed to overcome our own interpretations of where we were or are going to be.

 That, in better #simpleSPEAK, can mean, "our most Great Battle is paying attention to our abilities RIGHT NOW & listening to what our bodies are telling us."

Checking 'in' with the REAL us is not always the most enjoyable time spent, & most of us would rather do anything else [....even #MAKINGCOMICS seems doable in comparison :o)... ].

While the World around us can be in flames or not, the most meaningful character in our own stories is ourself, sometimes hogging the spotlight, while others slinking off, unoticed sometimes by even its caretaker, a well meaning person who, well, sometimes lacks the meaning of it all...

Whether playing chess or checkers, being a queen or a king, all must maintain a level of health past gamesmanship, taking time to set up the right moves so as to avoid any red flags while racing towards checkered flags.

It is a reminder we need sometimes, that which states 'we are not pawns in our own lives, & we are not boarded to being bored'...

With regular maintenance [ meals, sleep, interactions with organic voices ], we can avoid some dark nights, & who knows, maybe our creative output could include a few 'Dark Knights' or at least some 'Dark Horses'...

Being able to count on a few good opinions [...some call them friends :o) ] can go a long way in having some 2nd or 3rd hand eyes looking our way in ensuring all that needs to is getting done, and big bonus if you are able to take what is said about you with grains of salt without accusing others of having fiendish intentions.

This is hard, but so too is making comics, and having a clear healthy head to do it, a thirsty approach to proactive excersizes, & a built in audience of support is a well documented means to not only making it easier on yourself, but also being able to push others when they need it...

And sometimes, don't we all like hearing the sound of our voice saying to someone we care about, "(You're in..) Check, mate!"

 *********** Stay SAFE COMICS #117 ********** 

Stay SAFE comics - Comics Rule, But Have No Rules!

#ReadTradeShareCOMICS

Comic books & their forefather, the comic strip, had to do some heavy lifting back in the day...

As hard as it is to imagine a world without the undispensible tools of mass media & the internet, two feathers in the cap of humanity, those times are relatively recent, with most persons being able to find others quitely willingly offering to speak of the days without to get some perspective on our joint technological marchings over a scant few decades.

Comics WERE the entertainment, the fantasy, the science fiction, the vessel for imagination back in their 1st heydays, as, in a world without television, without 'talking' movies, without even widspread radio, comics we the indulgence for the creatively minded, & they didn't mind at all!

 

You should feel free, unhindered, when using the tools of the medium to tell your tales, tall or short, fiction or fact.

Liking to any form of expression or art, there are no real rules, except your own.Comics belong to their creators, at least initially, and so what you place on a page can be fixed to the place you are taking the reader, or can reach out in emotions or action to pull them in, it doesn't matter...

All that does is, hopefully the creator can help the reader find just the right sweet spot they'd want them to be in for the experience to work best...

With increased competition from up & coming companies like BOOM!, IDW, & DYNAMITE, mixed with an excellent [& getting better!!] IMAGE COMICS fixated on quality writings, DC has big plans of it's own....

It begins in DC's 2015 FREE COMIC BOOK DAY offering, DC COMICS DIVERGENCE one shot, and when the dust has settled after the craziness of APRIL & MAY 2015 THIS [BELOW] will emerge....
