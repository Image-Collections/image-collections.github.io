VALIANT COMICS DEAD-DROP 'GOR-GEOUS' IN MORE WAYS THAN ONE

CLASSY LOCAL ARTIST ADAM GORHAM, WHOS WORK WITH FEARLESS FRED OF 102.1 THE EDGE RADIO STATION FAME, ALONG WITH COUNTLESS OTHER PROJECTS, IS BREAKING THROUGH YET ANOTHER WALL OF THE COMICS MARKET...

Adam Gorham DEAD DROP s with underated IMAGE COMICS / MARVEL COMICS writer Ales Kot on a special new VALIANT COMICS title featuring action galore....

The new four-issue VALIANT COMICS miniseries is actually called DEAD DROP, and teams up the artist Adam Gorham, who ALSO has IMAGE COMICS experience with the title ZERO, with SECRET AVENGERS writer Ales Kot on a book taking a few chances, but it all seems to be adding up to a gamble very worth paying for...
