About AWA Studios Imprint Upshot Studios The Resistance, 

We SAY:

 

       Punching WAY above the medium's weight class, emotionally, J. Michael Straczynski and Mike Deodato Jr.'s The Resistance rips & tears with each story swing, leaving scars on those unfamiliar with the less spotlighted powers within comics....

        Those powers are, what can be found in just a few panels, can cause grimaces, grins, or suddenly grip a participant, lasting past a reader's dried tears, & can result in a convinced, committed, yet unconventional seeker of more of this [ to them ] unconventionally raw, authentic, & palpable story in, of all things, a comic...

'Just' paper, rolled into pages, some ink, a few staples, yet it can change anything or everything...'

The Resistance' has THAT feeling of, especially to those underexposed to the merits of the medium of comics, being something which should be vastly bigger than what it is...  

The mind races with all its potentials, unbound from its chains of the past, there is now a definite seduction of the industry to reach out and embrace each & every person who breath can help spread the word....

Use of a topic few have ever experienced previously...until now...aids the story to deeply isolate the many plots being precariously balanced, which, even under normal circumstances, would draw in the reader...

          ....the exact thing an industry could use more of as #COMICSreturn from an unheard of in 80 years shutdown due to the horrific topic of this engrossing read...

     Mike Deodato Jr. has had extensive runs as a Marvel comics artist drawing Amazing Spider-Man,  Avengers, Dark Avengers, The Incredible Hulk, & DC Comics Wonder Woman before taking up the cause of The Resistance from Upshot Comics, a comics imprint of the newly founded AWA Studios.

     As none of us are now as much an innocent in the ways of what has become the global face of the year 2020, we offer this warning, the next words you read will be chilling......

.

....and so it begins....

     

 

    Unknowing at the time of its creation, at least six months before its release date of 2020 [....SEE & LEARN MORE about that BELOW...], upon release it would be dosed in a much larger conversation about an unforseen tragic contamination, still the visuals & words were bounded together near-perfectly to deeply affect its readers thoughts & emotions...

     It is both without hope & because of it, we find, thanks to the strength of the artwork, words, & the placements of both, few-panel-characters resonate so deeply.....quickly finding their place echoing us still, pages & hours later.

      Mix in just a small needle's worth of 2020's pandemic flow of uneasiness into Upshot Comics The Resistance and soon it bleeds past the page, itself becoming a triggering super-spreader, hard to hold onto, blurred dueling-viruses, either one understandably difficult to think about, together, congealed, a heart-stoppingly too jagged personal experience for some...especially a parent.
