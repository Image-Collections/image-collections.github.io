Jessica Jones Preview

Jessica Jones Marvel P.I. Comic Comes Back!

Marvel Comics Jessica Jones Luke Cage Argue Their Way Through Life

For the 1st time in nearly 20 years Jessica Jones, the TV Show Netfllix made was a big hit, will be getting a new writer...

....and that writer won't be Brian Michael Bendis!

Originally the star of the comic titled 'ALIAS', then Marvel's first-ever regular-format mature readers imprint 'Marvel MAX', Jessica Jones was introduced as a complex easily aggitated person running her own Investigations company, where the comic got it's name....

From the starting F-bombs launched at an unsuspecting reader on Page 1, each page brought us closer to realizing what was being presented was indeed something fresh but dirty [for its time] from a place most expected the sqeaky-cleanliness of the past Marvel exploits to be forever #aTHING...

     Also, readers back then did not at all anticipate how well, within just a few panels, they'd come to know inside & out Ms Jones quirks as a hardened veteran of circumstances who's vulnerable side manifests itself as grumpiness at the very least....
