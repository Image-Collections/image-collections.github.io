Hello Fellow-ComicBook Lovers....

 
We wanted to let you know about  what is happening THIS WEEKEND, at our...
ANNUAL BREAD & HONEY 'INDEPENDENT COMIC-CON
both outside of the store  AND  in-the-PARK-itself  [on-the-island ] on ....
BOTH SATURDAY & SUNDAY,
which takes place at ONTARIO's 2nd or 3rd LARGEST FESTIVAL about a 15 minute drive outside TORONTO in Streetsville, MISSISSAUGA....and is FREE FOR CREATORS!!!
 
Drop by & see COMIC BOOK ARTISTS & WRITERS [ including ADONIS HOWARD & 'The Famous JILL' ] in action, and we encourage YOU to spread YOUR TALENTS as well by demonstrating that the ability to hold a pencil is INDEED an enhancing experience!!! We will also be TEACHING & PLAYING different games such as POKEMON, MAGIC, CHESS & others.....
 
[....aprox LOCATED @ ERIN MILLS PARKWAY / MISSISSAUGA ROAD / 401 HIGHWAY Area....]
