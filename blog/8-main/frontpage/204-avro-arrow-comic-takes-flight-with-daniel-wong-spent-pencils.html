Avro Arrow Comic Takes Flight with Daniel Wong & Spent Pencils

     Mississauga Comics Artist Daniel Wong, a local fan-favourite, unveiled today one of his latest projects, the 1st ISSUE of a HERITAGE MISSISSAUGA COMIC BOOK!......

     The easy to maneuver comic book, titled 'Grange Volume 1: Legend of the Arrow', is meant as a child-friendly introduction to a sometimes sadly left out page of our Canadian History, that of the Avro Arrow, itself a real-life heroic achievement in worldwide aviation....

     Joined by Cover Artist Vince Sunico & Writer/Letterer/Colourist incognito-person Madd Sketch, all of Spent Pencils fame, Daniel et al are looking forward to sharing it with you at an exclusive, yet open to the public, upcoming meet & greet which is sure to be a media-frenzied activity due to everyone [...including many dignitaries..] being in the shadow of a multi-tonned 'flightless friend'.....

     The comic will be launched at a special ceremony with the help of none other than the loooong-time Mayor of Mississauga Hazel McCallion on Saturday September 28th at 9am at the International Centre [Airport Road/Derry] during 'Doors Open Mississauga', though it'll run to 4pm for those especially fond of weekend rest.....

      Also in attendance will be the oversized guest of honour, a full-scale replica of the famous Canadian made 1950s Avro Arrow Interceptor Jet, who's story has captivated the nation for decades, and kept conspiracy theorists occupied just as long......
