Get SPOOKY in Streetsville [Mississauga, near Toronto] @ Image Collections 8th Annual Spooktacular Halloween Comic Fest

........

******   Join us this soon-to-be CREEPING-up 2013 weekend BEFORE Halloween, as on SCARY SATURDAY OCTOBER 26th Image Collections presents our 8th ANNUAL HALLOWEEN SPOOKTACULAR, complete with tons of events, a COSTUME PARADE, DRAWING LESSONS & FREE COMICS...now in 2 DIFFERENT LOCATIONS....

*****     COSTUMES are a must, with special activities throughout the day, games, balloon animals, face painting, story time, music time, cupcake decorating along with SEASONALLY specific Halloween CRAFTS: from jewelry making, to play-dough sculptures, painting and colouring...

*

***   KIDS [and parents] get into the Halloween spirit by engaging in the various creative and interactive activities offered.....along the traditional rewards of sweet FREE COMICS like SONIC, Batman, Ben-10, SPIDER-MAN, & Skyward!

***   ALSO: as an added BONUS, we will have MINI-COMICS for mini-people, with SUPER DINOSAUR, ADVENTURE TIME, SKETCH MONSTERS, VAMPLETS, & the ZOMBIE KID!!! We even have GIRL-friendly comic books like MY LITTLE PONY, ARCHIE, & POWERPUFF GIRLS!!!...

.....As always, supplies are limited, and the freebies, including cool LOOT-BAGS are...while supplies last, so arrive early!

**   This fun filled FAMILY EVENT caters to younger children (ages 3-8) and is the perfect introduction to a 100+ year-old literary & fun ART-FORM.... Dress your children up in their Halloween costumes and come out to Image Collections 2013 Halloween Comic Fest Spooktacular 2013!
