A MESSAGE TO ALL OF OUR CUSTOMERS........

====================================================================
---- In advance, THANK YOU....Thank you for being YOU, the person reading this, the person caring enough about others to be kind, considerate, and engaged in other peoples lives, not just now, but always.....

---- Sometimes being active before being asked is the right thing to do, and anticipating & listening is a large part of all of our jobs.......

---- Like the whole #COMICS industry [& LIFE] things have been changing, and so have we been, taking precautions at every level for our customers, our team, and the community in general......

---- If you have followed any of our postings on here or on our website, hopefully it has at least given an encouraging voice, however small, and helped you keep a vibe of caring about ALL that needs it right now....

---- That community is different now, & so are its priorities...For months now, "This is NOT buisness as usual" has been the norm.....For weeks now, "This is NOT LIFE as usual".....& for days, "This can't be happening..." has been proven wrong....

---- What these difficult hardships & adversities have done is what many have said was impossible, they have brought a new, more expansive definition to what many previously felt was closer in geography to the heart within them that they have for others......

---- Our community, your community, our village, town, county, city, provice, state, country, continent's community is much greater in distance, wider in scope,  yet in connection, smaller...and rounder than it was just a short time ago....

---- [It's?] For these reasons, among other considerations, in a decision that was extremely difficult to weigh, yet easier to make, Image Collections has closed it's physical doors while still keeping other doors open to serve you.

---- We will be keeping the lines of communication open to you via our website:

 www.imagecollections.ca   , posting updates on our pages whenever we can to keep all of you informed about the changing status of ....well, everything...

---- For our SUBSCRIBERS, we are continuing to hold your comics for you, please note: there will be publisher, distributor, industry, government changes which will affect almost all shipping dates moving forward....

---- We have been adapting, offering other options such as creating extra pull lists, curbside pick-ups, special deliveries, or other shipping options....all while keeping in mind we believe #PEOPLEfirst #COMICSsecond

---- You can reach us via phone at 905-542-8307 [= Leave us a message if we can't answer, and we WILL get back to you = ] You can also send us email or find us on social media at....
