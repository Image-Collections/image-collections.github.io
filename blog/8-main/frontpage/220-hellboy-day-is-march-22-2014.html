Hellboy Day is March 22, 2014

Hellboy Day is March 22, 2014

..... Yes, but really, this is HELLBOY DAY III @ Image Collections as we've already double-celebrated the red-skinned DARK HORSE COMICS character who's been the feature of two features, the star of 2 'talkies', or in simpler terms, the focus of two beautifully layered, easy-on-the-eyes, award winning, big-budget  MOVIES, HELLBOY (2004), &  HELLBOY II The Golden Army (2008) which snagged an ACADAMY-AWARD-nomination for Best Achievement in Makeup...

Image Collections recommends watching both HELLBOY films....AFTER [!!!] YOU READ the comics from whence he 'hatched'.  Come out on SATURDAY MARCH 22, 2014 to learn about the quick-witted 'humogo'-handed protector of 'everything right in the world', which can be stretched a li'll in his favour....

Hellboy is a comics-originating character created by writer-artist Mike Mignola who's real name, Anung Un Rama ("and upon his brow is set a crown of flame"), doesn't quite roll-off-the-tongue the same way...

Hellboy works for the BPRD, an international paranormal-battling agency, in a series of dark, noire-filled tales part X-Files meets Dracula / part Indianna Jones / part everything-but-the-kitchen-sink HORROR....

Hellboy is known throughout the comics world as "The World's Greatest Paranormal Investigator."
