AMAZING SPIDER-MAN #61 COVER

This, the long-awaited cover to The Amazing Spider-man #61 is finally here! Marvel has been teasing its Spider-man fans by only showing variant covers for this issue's final cover image, a partial picture of Spidey's new costume, which was set to debut in #62, the next one!

Because of a lot going on in the Spiderverse right now, both in the comic book, movie, & videogame, Marvel Comics is making it real easy to jump on to the storyline in Amazing, as Peter Parker has been getting a real workout.

#61 of the flagship Spider-man series, Amazing, will be taking the character in a new direction, with a new job, a new costume on the cover, and by #62, #63, #64, & #65, it seems like there will be a new web-slinger on the town!

Spider-man comics & back issues have been super hot lately with key issues finding collectors paying big money for old Spider-man keys, #50 #129 #194 #238 #252 #298 #300 #316 #361 & more!

Also, The Amazing Spider-man #55 has now gone to 4 printings already!  See our Blog for details or to book a copy!

You can pick up The Amazing Spider-man #61, #62, #63, #64, #65 from Marvel Comics right here @ Image Collections Canada's craziest comic store in Streetsville Mississauga Ontario Canada ...
