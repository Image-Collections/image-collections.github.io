AFTER VENOM 2 LET THERE BE CARNAGE BLACK WHITE AND BLOOD

 

 

Spiderman Villian Absolute Carnage Gets Black White & Blood Treatment!

 

It is always a thrilling ride whenever a literal spin-off of VENOM, formerly SPIDER-MAN's black costume of misfortune, come out to play, in this case as a special Carnage mini-series from Marvel Comics entitled Carnage Black White & Blood!  

The Carnage Black White & Blood title implys ketchup-based madness & falls in line with Marvel Comics other previous WOLVERINE Black White & Blood series begun in the year 2020.

 

CARNAGE BLACK WHITE AND BLOOD #1 (OF 4) GLEASON WEBHEAD VARIANT COVER MARVEL COMICS

(W) Donny Cates & Various (A) Sara Pichelli, Ken Lashley (CA) Sara Pichelli
