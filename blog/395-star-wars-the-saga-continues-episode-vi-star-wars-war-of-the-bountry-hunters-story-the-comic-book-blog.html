STAR WARS: WAR OF THE BOUNTY HUNTERS Story All This Summer!!

 

---"A LONG TIME AGO, IN A GALAXY FAR FAR AWAY...

....Picture THIS:

---It's the summer of 1980 &, if you can imagine it, you've just watched the biggest sequel in movie history, & even though it blew-your-mind & lived up to everything you wanted it to be. it left you hanging....

...hanging like never before!!!

"Nooooooooooooo!!!....",

No one would blame you for repeating that famous quote from this blockbuster 2nd installment of 'The Greatest Space Fantasy Film Of All Time', THE EMPIRE STRIKES BACK, as the movie shocked us all, just as it was meant to!

 

Now, 40 YEARS LATER, the post 'EMPIRE', pre-'RETURN OF THE JEDI, rest of the story can be told...but only in comics!

 

 

The 'WAR OF THE BOUNTY HUNTERS' Saga Of STAR WARS explains what happens when DARTH VADER, then JABBA THE HUTT, then the REBELS all spread the word to 'Hunt Han Solo', & everyone, especially Boba Fett's ilk of many new & old BOUNTY HUNTERS, hears it....

This will introduce new characters, rebel, empire, & old & new hired guns & may explain why some were never seen again after 'EMPIRE', as EVERYONE, even the 'good-gals-&-guys' are all trying to get Han!

It takes the 'teaming-up' of all 3 regular ongoing titles to tell it, 'Star Wars', 'Darth Vader', 'The BOUNTY HUNTERS' plus MORE!!!...
