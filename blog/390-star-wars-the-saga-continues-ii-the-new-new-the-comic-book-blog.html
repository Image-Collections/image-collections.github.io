STAR WARS THE SAGA CONTINUES II: THE NEW NEW! THE COMIC BOOK BLOG

STAR WARS THE SAGA CONTINUES EPISODE II: THE NEW NEW!  THE COMIC BOOK BLOG

 

More Star Wars movies needed for you?

That's what happens when we go a whole year without a new movie set in "a galaxy far far away..."

Partially Due 2 the popularity of 'The Mandalorian' Show, but MOSTLY due to STAR WARS COMICS with ALL-NEW ENGAGING STORIES being all-the-rage right now, DISNEY MARVEL Comics are doing an impressive job sticking to what the watchers & readers want, in canon Star Wars stories in a great [comics] format that allows the expansion of the Universe where Darth Vader wins!......or doesn't!!!

Darth Vader, the father of some important people that may have something to do with The Force & may use a lightsaber at some point in their life, is the perfect character in the perfect positioning in the latest Star Wars Comics to hit over the past year!

'Star Wars', 'Darth Vader', & 'Bounty Hunters' are three ongoing monthly Star Wars-set comics titles which all have true fans watering themselves (...we mean 'drooling', you may think elsewhere :o) for the next episodes, as all three have found steady homes on HOT COMICS & TOP TEN COMICS lists due to the excellent content & those who seek it!

Currently Darth Vader is on a lonely quest after a takedown unlike any ever seen in Star Wars lore, while a host of bounty hunters are sent after him, including one until recently only ever seen in the original Star Wars 1977 monthly comic, that also published by Marvel Comics, back in the golden days of a freshly minted space fantasy saga not yet labelled the greatest ever, but it still sure seemed like it to those fortunate enough to have been near a movie theater back then...
