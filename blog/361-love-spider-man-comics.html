LOVE SPIDER-MAN COMICS?

     Amazing Spectacular Web Friendly Neighbourhood Astonishing Sensational Spider-man just can't be beat in the comic readers nightstands & hot back issues, almost since first arriving in 1962 Spider-man has had his webbing all over all of us!

     Unlike another comics company flagship character, who also dresses in red & blue spandex, Peter Parker's appeal begins as an 'everyman', an identifiable character to many, long before any tights wearing superheroics showcases that distinct spider-like costuming.
