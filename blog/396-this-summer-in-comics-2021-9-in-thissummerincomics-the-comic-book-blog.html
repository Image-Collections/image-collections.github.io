SPIDEY COSTUME MALFULCTIONS

 

SPIDER-MAN has always had costume issues, from trying to sew or fix it, adding lights, tracers, or webbing under the armpits, to the stresses that must come from having to hide it from others all the time!

THIS isn't going where you think it is, for THAT story is coming later-on in this mini-countdown...

Peter Parker's biggest costume muck-up really WAS a muck, a living symbiote, which became an even bigger pain once it bonded to EDDIE BROCK & spawned more offspring symbiotes, such as CARNAGE & Scream, none of them even remotely thankful to its original host, SPIDEY...

Now 3 decades into this, VENOM has hit a milestone on his own....

Like THE PUNISHER before him, after many nasty run-ins in the flagship title 'THE AMAZING SPIDER-MAN', VENOM was spun off into his own title, one which, through the course of many numbered 'restarts & reboots' has achieved the publication of 200+ issues with his name on it, & now VENOM #200 is about to hit!

Just LOOK at THIS cover!!!


A time pivotal to everything VENOM-based or related being at its peak, with extraordinary writer bowing center stage!
