Free Comic Book Day is an annual promotional effort by the worldwide comic book industry to help bring new readers and creators into the exciting world of SEQUENTIAL ART....it occurs the FIRST SAT OF MAY every year....
 
On this date, we give away titles produced especially for F.C.B..Day by many different & varied publishers, who sell them to us at a reduced rate so that YOU can be introduced to a wide scope of genres, writing, art styles, & stories....
