Keep Calm And Binge Read Comics 2020

 

    Image Collections Wishes You & Yours An Effective, Safe, Caring, Compassionate, Productive, & #LOVE Reminding 'Unforeseen LIFE Disruption' That, Due To Health Concerns In Your Area, May Arise....

 

    Staying informed from reputable sources, knowing what is important information vs false, checking in frequently with family, friends, & those with greater needs , along with washing hands, & #WatchingOutForHeroes are ALL ways to make life easier....

 

     Please, when you can, strive to rise, being EXTRA neighbourly, understanding, & patient in these potentially stress-inducing times, when considerations of others can reflect the best of humanity's intentions to those watching us.... now, here & abroad, & to those who will be future readers of this history we are making....

 

Making sure we all individually live up to our own high standards of what we expect to be able to give to our families, our communities, & our world can make a huge difference, in what can be accomplished, the speed in which it can be done, & the beliefs that pave the pathways of what we deem possible from tomorrow's inspirations....

 

We wish you all peace, health, & the benefits that come with sharing in demonstrations of the best of hospitality without having to be hospitalized....but if you are, we wish you a speedy recovery & a notion to join us in thanking all those whose time, education, & extraordinary efforts are put into providing us the health care we are lucky to have access to....

 

Heroes can be found in the mythology, stories, & truths of the past, can be experienced by reading in texts, books, & comics, & they can be engaging, riviting, and motivating when we are exposed to them, but don't dare discount the influence real-LIFE heroism can have as well...

 

Even more-so than fiction's 'faster, stronger' notion of the heroic natures of Tarzan, The Phantom, Black Panther, or Superman, we need only look around us with our eyes wide open to unmask some truly remakable examples of what it means to be a 'human with heroism built-in'....

 

There are among us SUPER examples of HEROES, people we can admire for their unique achievements and noble qualities, who can combat adversity through feats of ingenuity, courage, & putting others ahead of themselves....

 

Like everything around us, every emotion we've ever felt, every experience of challenge or joy, this situation too will pass, and when the lessons have been learned from the hard pains it may bring, wouldn't it be better to look back upon it having been a partner in facilitating an easier future for us inclusively all to live in?...

 

Taking Care, Staying Safe, Attempting To #FlattenTheLine Whenever Possible , Helping When We Can, & Using The Discovery Of Reading The Limitless WORLD OF COMICS & GRAPHIC NOVELS, As Well As Using Our Own Lives To Continuously Be #WatchingOutForHeroes,....THAT'S How We All Can Rise, & Strive, To Better The Person We Wanted To Be, Once We've Observed & Learned What True Heroes CAN Be....

So.... Keep Calm And Binge Read Comics,

...Take Extra Care, Take In Extra #LOVE,

...Take In Extra Heroism,

...DON'T Take Your Health For Granted,

....I.C.

[...words by TM Maple Leaf ...]
