Marvin The Martian Comics Are Back!

Marvin The Martian Comics Are Back!

Marvin, that is 'Beloved Marvin The Martian', long-time LOONEY TUNES character who pre-dates almost everyone reading this posting, and favourite invading army leader of most of those pesky earthlings (...wouldn't want a capable one, right?), comes back to comics!

Along with Daffy Duck, super-pushy-salesperson, this comic, Looney Tunes #259 should, for those noted earlier born later who might be unfamiliar, answer the question, who is Marvin The Martian?

Marvin is an alien who is constantly trying to take over Earth with his invasion plans, and has been twarted by Duck Dodgers in the 241⁄2th century, a parody of Buck Rogers
