Happy Reading......IC :)

 

 

.History....Image Collections....& YOU...      [...by TM Maple Leaf...]

      Give us a moment, won't you, please....oh, look, you already have, and let us hugely say 'THANK YOU' for it, plus any others you may bless us with, including the time you are now spending reading THIS, these words, perhaps, minutes from now, to described as only a vastly challenging test of your patience while we wax on....

[.....DON'T WORRY....THERE'S COOL STUFF BELOW....INCLUDING A GALLERY OF 'BEST-OF' COMICS....WHICH MAKE IT ALMOST WORTH READING THE FORTHCOMING MEANDERING GIBBERISH...]

 

Time passes by, both taking & leaving in its wake....The passage of time, the presents of the past, and the presence of our own unique footsteps unprecedentedly define what has counted in our lives, and each of us make choices both personal and public which soon become who others come to know us as...

Most of us have experienced the joyful merits of time spent growing, morphing, and mapping out our own futures, the many times humourous reflections of examining our used ones, and even sometimes revisiting those moments we've come to know as 'learning curves' which have usually resulted in bettering ourselves more often than not....

Everyone leaves in the end, and it's the colour of the content mixed with a heaping portion of hopeful context left behind which is ultimately opinionated about by those yet to come....

Not all of you get the deserved credits for it, but make-no-mistake, YOU DO CHANGE THE WORLD....

Your impressions imprint themselves on each and every person you meet and, if you're lucky enough...many more you won't get the pleasure of discovering...

Surely [..hey! don't call me that!--Editor] you all are quite aware of this, as it's no great revelation or secret, yet it doesn't hurt as an extra reminder of how important you are meant to be.....

Us, on the other hand...well...we can only figure that Image Collections has had, for a time [24 years now ;o] a place in making some history of its own, and if you were a party to it, we hope it ended up for the best.

Through our years, many fine faces have come & gone, passing as they move on to the bigger & better adventures in Life, leaving in their wake bits which, when accumulated, have helped to mold us closer to what we've wanted to be...

COMING SOON will be, in this space, some reminders [BELOW] of Image Collections past, in pictures and in print, a reminder for us all of what we have passed along....

        How it contributes or contributed to how we are known, how you know us, or what colours are in your sky....

....well, that's YOUR HISTORY, and maybe we can be honoured if we, like you, rubbed off a bit....

Take Care Of Yourself,

Thanks For The Memories,

& Hopefully We'll See You Soon,

.....

FALL INTO READING

**********************************************************************************************************

 Image Collections fully recommends you listen to THAT VOICE in your head which insists on nudging you back towards READING COMICS AGAIN, rediscovering your LOVE OF THE COMICS MEDIUM, & not only picking up an issue or two for old times' sake, but ALSO SHARING IT WITH SOMEONE YOU LOVE....

....or 'SANDMAN' [ ...a mystical, all-encompassing, mind-altering take on Morpheus & your dreams almost overshadowed by his own sister, a woman literally COLD to the touch! ] ,...
