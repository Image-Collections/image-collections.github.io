Keep Calm And Binge Read Comics 2020

 

    Image Collections Wishes You & Yours An Effective, Safe, Caring, Compassionate, Productive, & #LOVE Reminding 'Unforeseen LIFE Disruption' That, Due To Health Concerns In Your Area, May Arise....

 

    Staying informed from reputable sources, knowing what is important information vs false, checking in frequently with family, friends, & those with greater needs , along with washing hands, & #WatchingOutForHeroes are ALL ways to make life easier....

 

     Please, when you can, strive to rise, being EXTRA neighbourly, understanding, & patient in these potentially stress-inducing times, when considerations of others can reflect the best of humanity's intentions to those watching us.... now, here & abroad, & to those who will be future readers of this history we are making....

 

Making sure we all individually live up to our own high standards of what we expect to be able to give to our families, our communities, & our world can make a huge difference, in what can be accomplished, the speed in which it can be done, & the beliefs that pave the pathways of what we deem possible from tomorrow's inspirations....

 

We wish you all peace, health, & the benefits that come with sharing in demonstrations of the best of hospitality without having to be hospitalized....but if you are, we wish you a speedy recovery & a notion to join us in thanking all those whose time, education, & extraordinary efforts are put into providing us the health care we are lucky to have access to....

 

Heroes can be found in the mythology, stories, & truths of the past, can be experienced by reading in texts, books, & comics, & they can be engaging, riviting, and motivating when we are exposed to them, but don't dare discount the influence real-LIFE heroism can have as well...

 

Even more-so than fiction's 'faster, stronger' notion of the heroic natures of Tarzan, The Phantom, Black Panther, or Superman, we need only look around us with our eyes wide open to unmask some truly remakable examples of what it means to be a 'human with heroism built-in'....

 

There are among us SUPER examples of HEROES, people we can admire for their unique achievements and noble qualities, who can combat adversity through feats of ingenuity, courage, & putting others ahead of themselves....

 

Like everything around us, every emotion we've ever felt, every experience of challenge or joy, this situation too will pass, and when the lessons have been learned from the hard pains it may bring, wouldn't it be better to look back upon it having been a partner in facilitating an easier future for us inclusively all to live in?...

 

Taking Care, Staying Safe, Attempting To #FlattenTheLine Whenever Possible , Helping When We Can, & Using The Discovery Of Reading The Limitless WORLD OF COMICS & GRAPHIC NOVELS, As Well As Using Our Own Lives To Continuously Be #WatchingOutForHeroes,....THAT'S How We All Can Rise, & Strive, To Better The Person We Wanted To Be, Once We've Observed & Learned What True Heroes CAN Be....

So.... Keep Calm And Binge Read Comics,

...Take Extra Care, Take In Extra #LOVE,

...Take In Extra Heroism,

...DON'T Take Your Health For Granted,

....I.C.

[...words by TM Maple Leaf ...]

STAY SAFE 0.5 It begins

STAY SAFE COMICS # 0.5

 

#StaySAFEcomics just make sense..... Stay Safe Comics Work, Too!

With Physical NEW COMICS Put On Hold, Millions Can Take Up The Cause...

"As A Calming Activity For Those Who's Nerves Are Fraying, Especially 4 'KIDS' under 85 [ :) ], #DRAWING & #WRITING Can Resume Sanity As A Progressive & Brain-Stablizing 'thing'......Do Them BOTH On The Same Page, You've Got COMICS!"

.....BUT 1st..... 

                      Everyone is hurting, we are trying to be proactive, finding ourselves a bit too reactive at times, and eyeballs need some time away from screens every now & then.....so what to do?

Gettin 'Jiggy With It' [puzzles], #Dicing [games], & old fashioned face to face conversations [at safe distancing range] can ALL help the hours pass, though eventually the need to just 'get away' mentally should be adressed before it manifests itself in unhealthy ["Get Away!!..."] ways....

So #READING can come into play, & this certainly includes reading comics in many forms, a medium, until recently, untapped in most North American's minds, fooled into believing it somehow didn't meet the criteria to handle pertinent information, at least not in their wheelhouse....

With this variable changing, and the thirst for correct information & experiences about ONE exact subject never having been greater ALL OVER THE WORLD, the need [& demand] has seldom captured the attention of most of humanity

As a calming activity for those who's nerves are 'frying', especially for kids under 85 [ :) ], drawing, & writing can resume sanity as a progressive & brain-stablizing 'thing'......Do them BOTH on the same page, you've got COMICS!

"So, reach inside yourself to put out the story you'd like, or were born to, share..."

The attention comics has gathered over the past twenty years, due to hit movies, TV Shows, a massive influx of new comic titles, each building readership across non-traditional lines & ages, is off the charts when it comes to quality, diversity, and a newly found place of rocketing respect....

So a perfect storm to strike lightning, and if ever there was a subject to unite a world where so many ALREADY have found important articulation in sequential art, a tsunami-acting virus health crisis might be it....

Stay Safe comics can be a rallying cry for sharing one's insights no matter our age, race, gender, or border....

Reaching out as a community to motivate others to tell the stories the future will be talking about for a long time, helps alleviate the untrue notion that powerful tools are not within our grasp, even when our arms are restricted in fighting an enemy we can not see....

Stay Safe comics can be a rallying cry for sharing one's insights no matter our age, race, gender, or border....

Everyone can find their voice, unrestricted from all but the oldest storytelling tools [ paper & pencil ], yet shared, using the mightiest of our constructions [ the internet ], as a place of release & redemption...

In rebellion against something that held back our physical movement, but not our collective consciousness, a time where we held our breath, because we feared not being allowed to breathe, learning indeed, that together, even apart, we, as one, sighed in relief on a page, as, locked-down, so many of us were finally able to capture our true 'Raison d'être'....

So reach inside yourself to put out the story you'd like, or were born to, share...

Making Comics is certainly the easiest, or most complex of the communication arts....

It is up to the maker, the decider, the mindful who can play with the page in ways unimaginable to a casual onlooker who doesn't yet realize the potentials of its bare ingredients...& what can grow from a blank page...

The Stay SAFE comic:

......A place for anything & everything felt during this huge unintended time out....

....makes moving forward with a plan an awareness we've seldom ALL shared....

or cared to....
