FANTASTIC 3?

                          

.

THREE'S COMPANY For The Company's Flagship Title.... 

FANTASTIC FOUR, the debut title that ushered in the MIGHTY MARVEL EXPERIENCE way back in the 1960s, is going to be a rocky place to hang your 'readers hat' from now on, as it is soon to be a'changin....

.

Marvel Comics will be killing off one of the FANTASTIC FOUR this spring, blazing way for an all-new team featuring a replacement character who's name isn't 'WOLVERINE' [...whew!...]!

.

WHO will die?...and HOW could they do this?....WHERE does it happen?......WHAT are the ramifications of such an event?.....SHOULD you open the polybagged issue?

.

For answers to some of these questions, pick up your very own personal copy of 'FANTASTIC FOUR #587!

[...and keep the tissue handy ;o} ]
