Amazing Spider-man #55 PATRICK GLEASON

 

Amazing Spider-man #55 PATRICK GLEASON

 

Nick Spencer Spiderman Cover #55 Amazing

Spider-man Hits A Cover Full, & Colourful Cover

 

Marvel comics gives readers & collectors of hot comics new reasons to care again about Spider-man (or spiderman for those too busy to find the hyphen button on the keyboard -- you KNOW who you are! :)!

Patrick Gleason cover webbing silhouette stylistic white on black cover background is just so enticing to the eye of nearly everyone who sees it!

As well as being the #55 cover artist on Marvel's flagship Spider-man Amazing title, Patrick Gleason, blessed to have been drawing comics professionally for the past 20 years (almost all in the 'big leagues' of Marvel Comics & / or DC Comics!), has teamed up with Nick Spencer drawing comics interior pages as well....including this one! :o) 

Amazing Spider-man #55 is one of the key comics issues within Nick Spencer's acclaimed comic 60+ so-far issue run on the title as Spider-man's current main writer.

(Yes, we just seemingly performed some sort of math gymnastics to those Spider-man movie fans not in the know when it comes to Marvel Comics challenging numerational exploits throughout Marvel history ....more on that later.)

Not that the latest Spider-man storyline doesn't already have a LOT going on, with shake-ups galore & former villians 'a-snore' hitting Peter Parker & his unique supporting cast hard, some tense expectations & plenty of storyline baiting is finally paying off for Spidey followers of all ages!

Preferred to most readers, in our 30+ years of comics retailing experience anyways, Nick Spencer has taken the 'tease them a bunch, then make it payoff' approach in writing the webslinger.

Lots of readers of The Amazing Spider-man ongoing Volume 5 (..."Whaa??", we'll explain later.. :o) have really embraced his ability to foreshadow story, throw hints near & far, & provide reliably quintessential Peter Parker stories to a well educated, by comics & movies, SPIDEY audience.
