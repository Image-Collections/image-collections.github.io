ALL AGES FUN FAMILY READING is back in style....

It's worth mentioning, ALL-AGES COMICS are very popular right now, so you may wish to pick one up for a youngster in your life...

 

Be a pal and give a memorable comic book, their first one...and while you're at it, why not READ it to them as well. They will treasure the experience forever, and, as you introduce them to the art of reading, and watch their eyes light up at the sight of what you ALSO remember fondly, we bet you will just as much...

 

HELPUL HINTS FOR ALL AGES COMICS BUYING:

 

Today there exists more easy reading tools for the casual reader than ever before, and what better way to encourage the perusal of words than...comic books!

 

 

SOME OF THE BEST ALL-AGES COMICS BEING PUT OUT ARE:


[[....pick up one for a YOUNGSTER IN YOUR LIFE, it's worth spending a couple bucks...it will pay off for you with many brownie points, help them discover the art of reading, not to mention aquiring a new padawan who looks up to you :o]]

  • TINY TITANS [dcKids]... perfect for ANY AGE, large panels, simple words, and FUN stories!
  • DC SUPERFRIENDS [dcKids]... kid-friendly HEROICS with no violence(!!!)....
  • ARCHIE [archie-duh!]... the high school 'player' just got MARRIED....TWICE!
  • TOY STORY [boom!kids]... continuing the movie magic with all-new stories!
  • UNCLE SCROOGE [boom!kids]... penny-pinching duckiness!
  • SONIC the HEDGEHOG [archie comics]... old-school video madness!
  • CARS [boom!kids]... Lightning McQ & friends, are back in all new on-going series!
  • THE MUPPETS [boom!kids]... one of the BEST WRITTEN comics on the market in true retro-style craziness!
  • SABRINA the TEENAGE WITCH [archie]... still the wacky hijinx you remember!
  • SUPERHERO SQUAD [marvel]... Marvel's mini-mites in original post-TV adventures!
  • THE INCREDIBLES [boom!kids]... everyone's favourite superhero family, now monthly!
  • BETTY & VERONICA [archie comics]... Archie's sometimes love-birds in their own long-running series!

These are just SOME of the wonderful reading that awaits any young comic fan, check out the web, Image Collections, or ANY comic store for many more....THANKS!
