CORONAVIRUS COMICS CORONAVIRUS UPDATE

---------SORRY, FANS......we are VERY SORRY TO HAVE TO TAKE UP YOUR TIME, YOUR VERY VALUABLE TIME, TO PUT ASIDE THE ONGOING, VERY IMPORTANT EXPRESSIONS OF A GREAT WORLD-BONDING MEDIUM....COMICS...TO SHARE SOME LOWLY THOUGHTS on THE HIGHEST PRIORITY ITEM OF OUR TIME, TODAY, THIS EXACT MOMENT......

UPDATE ----> #MARCH200 ---- MODERN HISTORY HAS NEVER SEEN A MONTH LIKE THIS ONE..... MAYBE that IS A BIT OVERBOARD, BUT the WHOLE WORLD IS has BEEN STRUCK, PUSHED into AN UNWANTED SPOTLIGHT that FUTURE HISTORY-WRITERS will be DEVOTING MUCH SPACE to......

    HUMANITY IS UNDER PRESSURE TO SHOW it's COMBINED WORTH VALUES ITSELF AS A WHOLE JUST AS MUCH as it SEEMS TO INDIVIDUALLY, & THAT OUR SUBJECTIVE DIVISIONS DON'T SUBTRACT, but INSTEAD ADD MULTIPLE LEVELS OF CHARACTER, INFLUENCE, & IDENTITY that AN ENTIRE SPECIES CAN BE PROUD OF.....OR NOT.

     CONTINENTS, COUNTRIES, COMMUNITIES are PLAYING THEIR ROLES, EVERY DAY OUTSTRETCHED ARMS, BUDGETS, AND PATIENCE ARE BEING PUT TO THE TEST by the CORONAVIRUS in CREATING THE PLOTS, SCRIPTS, & VISUALS OF TOMORROWS ANALIZATIONS OF TODAY.....

      WILL THESE HANDS BE GIVING OR TAKING, PUSHING AWAY OR PULLING TOWARD?.....AND WHAT TYPES OF ARMS WILL THEY BE REACHING FOR?.....WILL THE FIGHT TO BE A TOGETHER, TOGETHER MAKE MAKE THESE HOURS TRULY OURS TO EMBRACE?...

      PERHAPS THE ONLY TRUTH IS, THE TEST WIILL RESOLVE ITSELF to THE FACT WE WILL GET THERE, WHEREVER THAT TAKES US, WHETHER WE LIKE IT OR NOT, AS ONE, FOR, UNLIKE MOST OF OUR PAST CONFLICTS, THIS TIME, WE ARE NOT THE EMEMY [E-ME-MY]....WHICH LEADS US, STRONGER FOR IT, INTO A CHALLENGE WHERE TOGETHER WE REALIZE, WE ARE NOT THE ENEMY........
