SPIDERGWEN JOINS NEW 'ALL-GENDER' LINEUP OF FRIENDLY READING at IMAGE COLLECTIONS...

[.....sure it SOUNDS corny, but extraordinary long-time writer DAN SLOT has a unmistakable knack for challenging himself with transforming the, at first glance, simplest of ordinary story ideas into complex, multilayered, delightful plots ripe for reading.....see 'SPIDER-ISLAND' [everyone in Manhattan gets SPIDER-Powers?!?] & 'SUPERIOR-SPIDER-MAN' [Peter Parker & Dr Ock--, er...just READ IT, it's Great!!]....]
