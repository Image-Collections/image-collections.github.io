 THE DARK KNIGHT RISES Movie plus Special BAT-RELATED ACTIVITIES B-4

   

          

'DC's NEW-52' relaunched, rebranded, and re-restored to greatness JUSTICE LEAGUE features a starting-from-scratch [...& we mean the "NONE OF THE HEROES HAVE EVEN MET YET"-type of scratch that unavoidably wants to be itched!!!..:o] storyline just begging to be picked-up by 'newbie' readers brought in through cool concepts, dimly-lit DARK KNIGHT movies [...shadow-puppets, anyone?..], and the mostly earned and 'payoffable' 'NEW-52' hype and hoopla...
