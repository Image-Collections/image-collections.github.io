THE BEST-SELLING COMIC OF 2011

[....you heard it HERE @ Image Collections first :o],

 

with sales possibly higher than any comic of the past 8 years, the

NEW DC-52's JUSTICE LEAGUE #1 welcomes back to monthly comics the

smashing style of modern North American superhero prototype artist,

.

JIM [ X-men, Batman:Hush ] LEE....

. 

. 

Comic 'Heroic Reboots' have paved the road

of the industry's history seemingly forever,

sometimes sparkling [...like DC's 1980s

'CRISIS ON INFINITE EARTHS' fixer-story,

...John Byrne's NEW AGE Superman classic

'MAN OF STEEL', ...George Perez's perfect

Wonder Woman....1990s Valiant-Gold Key,

Marvel's 1960s Launch, 2000's 'ULTIMATES',

& game-changer 'CIVIL WAR'....being just

some prime examples], while others

remaining nameless here, proved more

'speedbump-roadkill-retread'....

. 

. 

While revision is sometimes necessary,

as, unlike almost any other medium, sorting

through thousands of popular stories spread

over decades of monthlies, one-shots, and

graphic novels provided to a thirsty public

eager to be quenched with a reality-based continuity becomes a daunting

task very quickly...

. 

. 

It's no coincidenceTV's longest running prime-time entertainment program,

'The Simpsons', recently beating out 'Gunsmoke' for that title [both of whom

have had comic incarnations featuring new stories true to their source material,

GUN-over 30 issues/ THE SIMPSONS has over 160+ comics and counting...],

doesn't concern itself with integrating its stories together [..Homer fired again?]

as to do so would involve hours of fustration, and limit future storylines....

.

.

Hence sequential art's heroic tendencies to...

paint itself into a corner, both as a challenge for its

creators, and testament to its uniqueness as a 

fulfilling storytelling medium... 

. 

. 

   Therefore, it is not only commendable, but close to

unbelievable that DC COMICS, by way of loved writer

Geoff [FLASH, GREEN LANTERN REBIRTH] Johns,

would begin it's pursuit of expectant reader's acclaim not

by focussing on the minute, but instead roll out

a complete revising of it's entire 'big-gun' line-up in...

 

'JUSTICE LEAGUE #1' as the

FIRST TITLE to hit the stands on AUGUST 31st,....

 

....leaving it's always opinionated yet adoring audience a

full week to mull over before the other #1's appear!

.

.
