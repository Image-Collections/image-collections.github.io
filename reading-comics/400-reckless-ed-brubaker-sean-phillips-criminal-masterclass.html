RECKLESS, ED BRUBAKER & SEAN PHILLIPS CRIMINAL MASTERCLASS

Ed Brubaker, winner of 7 Eisner (Acadamy) Awards, known for consistantly high quality writings ('CRIMINAL', 'GOTHAM CENTRAL', 'CATWOMAN', 'FATALE', 'FADE OUT'), is currently co-creating:
 
 
A Graphic Novel Series By Ed (Marvel's 'Winter Soldier') Brubaker & Sean Phillips:
( PLOT: Hard-edged Gritty 1980s L.A. set crime series featuring devastating hard-luck situations, Twisty-Stories, & Rock-Solid-Characters!)
 
RECKLESS Graphic Novels are sold only in 'collected' format, perfect for long BINGE-READING sessions that make you think!
  For perspective, think a literary version of a 'The Equalizer' / 'Miami Vice' / '100 Bullets' / 'Punisher' / mix! 
 Sooooo GREAT, from the best writer/artist team working in comics today, ED BRUBAKER, the writer of 2004's retro-revamped 'CAPTAIN AMERICA: WINTER SOLDIER' comic that lead to the movies, & SEAN PHILLIPS, acclaimed artist of 'CRIMINAL', 'CONSTANTINE', 'FATALE', & 'MARVEL ZOMBIES 2'!!!
 
 
 
 
RECKLESS BY BRUBAKER & PHILLIPS Graphic Novels are not to be missed for any action adventure crime reader interested in learning about the human condition under diress...
