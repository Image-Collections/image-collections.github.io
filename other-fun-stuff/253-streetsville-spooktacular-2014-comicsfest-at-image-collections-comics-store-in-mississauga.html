Streetsville Spooktacular 2012

.........

Thanks for taking time out to visit us, as we hope YOU are able to do IN PERSON one day also @ our 21 YEAR OLD STORE in Streetsville, Mississauga.......

.....................

Streetsville comes ALIVE this October with it's Annual 2012 STREETSVILLE SPOOKTACULAR Kids-Fest Celebration, a fun experience for both parents and children...

This year's Event will be held SATURDAY OCTOBER 27th 2012, and Image Collections, Streetsville's COMIC BOOKS and CARDS store, a founding sponsor, and every year participant, will be back with activities, music and prizes....

A limited amount of select 'TREATS' will be given out @ Image Collections [...beside I.D.A @ Tannery Street...] on this date...

As is the custom, Image Collections will be looking for those decked out with COMIC-THEMED attire and SUPERHERO COSTUMES....

Come out to the STREETSVILLE SPOOKTACULAR 2012 and join Image Collections for some GREAT FUN....
