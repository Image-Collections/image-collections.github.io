Inside each person is some LOVE trying to get out, let's make it easier....

Lets make that easier for everyone...

Spiderman 2020 holds many a mystery....what is in store for spider-man in 2020?

 Spider-man appears in a few different Marvel comics, with some still published today, such as 1960s The Amazing Spider-man [ soon to reach Amazing #850 ], 1970s begun Peter Parker The Spectacular Spider-man [ over 300 issues ], 1980s started Web Of Spider-man [180], the 1990s Todd McFarlane drawn / written Spider-man, & 2000s released Mark Millar & Chip Zdarsky titiles, each add a little more to the Spider-man comic mythos....

Other titles over the years included the Spider-man Team Up title Marvel Team Up #1 to #150 [....that the next month became Web Of Spider-man ], the easy reading, beginning to read comics, & Electric Company TV Show sponsored title Spidey Super Stories #1 - #57 [ complete with a helpful Mr E-Z Reader to encourage absorbing the mostly one syllable words ], the quarterly Spider-man Unlimited #1 - #22 [ it started with a bang, the Maxium Carnage story ], reprint title Marvel Tales Staring Spider-man which ran from #1 - #291, The Superior Spider-Man #1 - #33 was a Doctor Octopus fueled ramped-up tale that left almost everyone reading breathless by the time it was done....
